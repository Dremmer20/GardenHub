Knowledge & Harvest

Tips for our garden and ideas for zero-waste distribution.

Crop Care Guides

Sweet Corn & Peppers

Sweet corn requires consistent moisture and high nitrogen levels. Keep an eye out for pests as the ears begin to develop. Peppers prefer warm soils and deep, infrequent watering cycles.

Squash, Pumpkins, & Beans

Squash and pumpkins need plenty of room to vine out. Watch for squash bugs under the leaves. Pole and bush beans will help naturally fix nitrogen back into our soil plot.

Our Zero-Waste Goal

To ensure 100% of our harvest serves our community, we are utilizing a multi-tiered distribution strategy:

  • Tier 1 (Fresh Distribution): Immediate packaging for local food pantries and families in need right after Sunday huddles.
  • Tier 2 (Preservation): Excess produce will be directed to group canning, freezing, or drying workshops.
  • Tier 3 (Composting): Any unharvestable organic matter goes straight into our campus compost bins to fuel next season's soil.
